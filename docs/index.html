About RocketSys

We all know Ro-Aviation, we all know the Ro-Hotel industry... why isn't there something new and exciting? That's what Sublivion and his team were wondering. We came up with an idea; creating Ro-Space accesible for anyone and everyone. That's where V. I and V. II came kicking in. Simple but advanced rockets within the ROBLOX platform.

Well Documented

All versions have been well-documented using GitHub, although we came to the notice that people still found it hard, this is why we've started on making a way better, highly detailed documentation for RocketSys V. III.

Super Fast

Once you get the hang of it, the system is very easy to install, which makes your space-agency to it's best state as fast as possible!
